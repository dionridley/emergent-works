**Nashid — Digital Literacy Mentee**

My one on one with Army, has an amazing passion and dedication which makes every session engaging and productive. Each day at the Cohort is an opportunity to absorb knowledge and grow, making the learning experience not just educational but deeply inspiring.

Emergent Works isn't just a program, it's a community. The support I have received from the team has been phenomenal, from fellow students during the Community Sessions & Sharing Circles to the guidance of dedicated mentors like Tine.
