## Key Observations for Redesign

1. **No embedded forms** -- The page has no contact/partnership inquiry form. The "Connect With Us" button links to the About Us page rather than a dedicated partnership form.
2. **Partner logos have no alt text** -- All 14 partner logos use empty alt attributes, making them unidentifiable from HTML alone. Partner names would need to be sourced from the organization.
3. **Social media links are Wix defaults** -- All social links point to Wix accounts rather than Emergent Works' actual social accounts. These need to be updated.
4. **No specific partner names listed** -- Beyond "Center For Community Alternatives" (CCA), no other partner names appear as text. The remaining partners are only represented by their logos.
5. **Single testimonial** -- Only one partner testimonial (CCA) is featured.
6. **Strong visual hierarchy** -- Uses contrasting section backgrounds (white/cream vs dark green) to create clear visual separation.
7. **Partnership categories are well-defined** -- Four clear partnership types with concise descriptions.
8. **CTA opportunities** -- Only two CTAs on the page: "Learn More" (scroll) and "Connect With Us" (links to About Us). A dedicated partnership inquiry mechanism would strengthen the page.
